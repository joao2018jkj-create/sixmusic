Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Six Music collects, stores and uses data.

1. Information We Collect

Six Music may collect server IDs, user IDs, playlists, favorites, music history and server settings.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used to provide features such as playlists, favorites, queue persistence and better user experience.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only used for operational purposes. We do not sell or share user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Six Music uses Discord API, Lavalink, Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud and Deezer.

5. Data Removal

Users may request removal of stored data through our support server.

6. Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect stored data, but absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may change over time to reflect updates, improvements or legal requirements.
