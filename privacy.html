Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Six Music collects, stores, protects and uses user data to provide its services and features.

1. Information We Collect

Six Music may collect and store server IDs, user IDs, custom playlists, favorite tracks, playback history, queue data, language settings and Spotify authentication tokens (when users connect their Spotify account).

2. Spotify Account Connection

Users can connect their Spotify account through the official Spotify OAuth system. Six Music only requests read-only permissions and cannot modify playlists, account settings or any Spotify content.

3. Token Storage & Security

Spotify tokens are stored locally and securely for operational purposes only. These tokens are used exclusively to read playlists and improve compatibility. Users can remove their stored tokens at any time using the /spotifylogout command.

4. How We Use Data

Data is used to provide features such as persistent queues, playlists, favorites, autoplay, playback recovery, smart search improvements and Spotify integration.

5. Third-Party Services

Six Music interacts with third-party services including Discord API, Spotify API, Lavalink, YouTube, SoundCloud, Deezer, Bandcamp and Audius. Each service has its own privacy policy and data practices.

6. Data Sharing

Six Music does not sell, share or distribute user data to third parties. All stored data is used strictly for the operation of the bot and its features.

7. Data Removal

Users may request removal of stored data through our support server. Spotify tokens can be instantly removed directly by the user using /spotifylogout.

8. Security Measures

Six Music applies reasonable security measures to protect stored data, including local token isolation, backup protection and restricted access. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to reflect system improvements, legal requirements or new integrations. Continued use of Six Music means acceptance of the updated policy.

Need help?

If you have privacy-related questions, security concerns or data removal requests, contact our support team.
