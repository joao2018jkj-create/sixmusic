Why choose Six Music?

🎯 Advanced Music Matching

Six Music uses an advanced scoring system to detect the best possible track, reducing wrong versions, remixes and unrelated results.

🟢 Spotify Integration

Spotify is used as a smart music catalog for tracks, albums and playlists, with support for user login through /spotifylogin.

🔐 Spotify User Login

Users can connect their Spotify account securely with read-only permissions and disconnect anytime using /spotifylogout.

▶️ YouTube Playlist Support

YouTube playlists are handled with exact video links, improving playlist accuracy and reducing incorrect metadata-based replacements.

🔁 Smart Fallback System

Six Music can search through SoundCloud, simplified queries and YouTube fallback to find difficult tracks with better reliability.

💾 Persistent Queue

Queue, volume, loop mode and history can be saved, helping sessions recover after restarts or temporary connection issues.

💜 Favorites System

Save your favorite songs and play them again instantly whenever you want.

📂 Playlist System

Create, save, load and manage custom playlists directly inside Discord.

⚡ Autoplay & Prefetch

Keep the music going automatically and prepare the next track in advance for smoother transitions.

🔄 Lavalink Recovery

Automatic Lavalink reconnect and queue recovery help keep playback stable during temporary node issues.

🎛️ Interactive Panel

Control playback with buttons, queue navigation, loop controls and real-time panel updates.

Spotify Security & Privacy

Six Music uses read-only Spotify permissions. The bot cannot modify playlists, change account settings, follow users, post content or control a Spotify account. Connected users can remove their stored Spotify token at any time with /spotifylogout.
