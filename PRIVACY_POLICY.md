# Six Music Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Six Music collects, stores and uses information when you interact with the bot.

By using Six Music, you agree to this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

Six Music may collect and store:

- Server IDs
- User IDs
- Saved playlists
- Favorite songs
- Music history
- Server settings
- Queue persistence data

We do not collect passwords, emails or private messages.

---

## 3. How We Use Data

The collected data is used only to provide bot functionality, including:

- Saving playlists
- Saving favorites
- Maintaining queue persistence
- Improving autoplay features
- Enhancing user experience
- Fixing bugs and improving stability

---

## 4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only used for operational purposes.

We do not sell, share or distribute user data to third parties.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Six Music uses external services such as:

- Discord API
- Lavalink
- Spotify
- YouTube
- SoundCloud
- Deezer

These services may have their own privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data Removal

Users may request removal of stored data by contacting support.

Some data may be automatically deleted when no longer necessary.

---

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time to reflect improvements, security changes or legal requirements.
